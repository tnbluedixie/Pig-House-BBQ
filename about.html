About Us

The Pig House BBQ has been the #1 BBQ-selling restaurant in Jackson, Tennessee since its opening in 1987. We never fail to satisfy consumers with our menu of delicious meats, sandwiches, and drinks.

The Pig House is currently run by David, Keith, and Tyler Clifton. We are and have always been a family business, which makes our restaurant so unique.

Our Backstory

We have always wanted to serve every customer to the best of our ability! We always try to put our name out there so that everyone can know about and try our BBQ for themselves, just to see how true the rumors really are!
